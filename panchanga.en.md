# Panchanga

## Nakshatras

Nakshatras are the 27 lunar constellations used in the Hindu calendar.

- Ashwini
- Bharani
- Krittika
- Rohini
- Jyeshtha

## Masa

Masa refers to the lunar month in the traditional calendar.

- Chaitra
- Vaishakha
- Jyeshtha
- Ashadha

## Tithis

Tithi is a lunar day.

| Tithi Name | Number |
|------------|--------|
| Pratipada  | 1      |
| Dwitiya    | 2      |
| Tritiya    | 3      |
| Chaturthi  | 4      |
